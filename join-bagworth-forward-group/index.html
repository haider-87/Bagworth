Join Bagworth Forward Group

The Bagworth Forward Group is comprised of local residents who are dedicated to the continued improvement of our village. We meet to discuss matters affecting Bagworth, consider suggestions for improvements, and undertake practical projects. Our focus is on taking positive steps, whether that involves enhancing our shared environment, arranging community events, or ensuring that local views are represented.

If you have some time available and a desire to be involved, we would be very pleased to welcome you to our group. Your input and energy could be invaluable.

Please get in touch at contact@bagworth.org.uk or through our Facebook page.

We encourage you to join us in working together to ensure Bagworth remains a pleasant place to live.
